    Class PhysicsSolver

    Physics constraint solver for contact resolution and collision response.

    class PhysicsSolver;

    The PhysicsSolver implements sequential impulse solving with contact persistence and stability algorithms. It handles both velocity and position corrections to prevent penetration and provide realistic collision response, sometimes accepting small hits in performance.
